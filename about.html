About Tecseal LTD

Tecseal was founded in 1986 and is the work of its untiring creator, Brian O'Byrne. Today Tecseal excels as a unique product sourcing, problem solving and design service.

Tecseal is operated by its two directors Brian O'Byrne and his partner Niamh Mcgovern. Together they bring experience and panache that leaves every job done in exceptional fashion.
